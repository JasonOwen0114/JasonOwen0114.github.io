1904-1955

The Rolls-Royce brand was founded by Charles Rolls and Henry Royce in Manchester, England.The first six-cylinder Rolls-Royce model, the Silver Ghost, was introduced. During World War I, Rolls-Royce produced aircraft engines for the British military.The Phantom I model was introduced, which would become one of the company's most iconic models.Rolls-Royce acquired Bentley, another British luxury car manufacturer. During World War II, Rolls-Royce produced aircraft engines and tanks for the British military.The Silver Cloud model was introduced, which would become another iconic model for the brand.

1965-2021

Rolls-Royce acquired Bristol Siddeley Engines, a British aero-engine manufacturer.Rolls-Royce was nationalized by the British government due to financial difficulties. The Silver Spirit model was introduced, which marked a new era of modernization for the brand.Rolls-Royce was acquired by the German car manufacturer BMW. The Phantom VII model was introduced, marking the brand's first new model under BMW ownership.The brand's first SUV model, the Cullinan, was introduced.Rolls-Royce announced its plans to transition to electric vehicle production by 2030.
