PHANTOM

SERIES II

The Phantom Series II is a luxury car produced by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, which is the successor to the original Phantom model. The Phantom Series II was introduced in 2012 and received a facelift with minor exterior and interior design changes, as well as some technological upgrades. The car is known for its elegant design and luxurious features, including a spacious cabin with hand-stitched leather upholstery, a panoramic sunroof, and a state-of-the-art sound system. It also features advanced safety and comfort features, such as adaptive cruise control, night vision assistance, and a heads-up display. Under the hood, the Phantom Series II is powered by a 6.75-liter V12 engine, which produces 453 horsepower and 531 pound-feet of torque. It is capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.8 seconds and has a top speed of 149 mph. Overall, the Phantom Series II is a top-of-the-line luxury car that offers a smooth and comfortable ride with exceptional performance, advanced technology, and exquisite craftsmanship.
