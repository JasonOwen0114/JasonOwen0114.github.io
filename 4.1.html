Rolls-Royce leather is a work of exquisite artistry.

There are endless ways in which a Rolls-Royce outshines all other motor cars in existence. The same can be said of our leather, which is, without question, the finest in the world. We have worked alongside our trusted farmers for nearly two decades, raising bulls in impeccable conditions with the utmost care for their wellbeing. The humid, temperate surroundings of their home keeps their skin healthy and supple, while the lush grass they graze is full of immunity-boosting nutrients and antioxidants.

Only the highest quality leather meets the Rolls-Royce standard.

Meets the Rolls-Royce standard.

The selection process is rigorous. Using chalk markings, our leather experts identify and eliminate any scars, insect bites or other imperfections, no matter how minor. However, this initial approval is only the first step. Once the perfect material is selected, it’s put through intensive measures that test the leather’s overall performance and longevity. One such measure involves placing the leather in a temperature-controlled chamber that’s chilled to exactly -10°C, then rapidly heated to 100°C degrees.

Our Variant

Nappa leather is a type of full-grain leather that is made from the top layer of the animal's hide. It is soft and pliable and is commonly used in luxury cars and high-end accessories. Nappa leather is often finished with a thin layer of polyurethane to increase its durability and resistance to water and stains. Nappa leather is known for its fine grain, soft texture, and luxurious feel..

Ostrich leather is a type of exotic leather that comes from the hides of ostriches. It is prized for its unique quill pattern, which is caused by the follicles from which the ostrich's feathers grow. Ostrich leather is known for its softness, durability, and distinctive appearance. It is commonly used in high-end fashion accessories and luxury car interiors.

Buffalo leather is a high-quality and luxurious type of leather used by Rolls-Royce, a renowned British luxury car manufacturer. It is sourced from the hides of the water buffalo, which is known for its strength and durability.
